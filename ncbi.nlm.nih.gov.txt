Download primerList.xlsx(https://github.com/FormFillerPlus/FormFillerPlus/blob/main/primerList.xlsx) to your local machine. 
Open FireFox web browser and go to: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/tools/primer-blast/index.cgi
Click "Ctrl + s", then a file selection window will pop up. 
Choose the downloaded Excle file. 
Form Filler Plus will submit the genomic targets to primer blast to design primer
When all the primers are designed, the candidate primers will saved in a text file.
